Football

Our Football in Westgress

Curriculum Intent 

Football, football, football. It’s the best sport in the world and dominates the thoughts and feelings of so many. As Europe’s biggest sporting retailer, we’re proud to bring football to the masses, with a mammoth offering of football boots, kits and equipment, provided by football’s top brands. Our football shop covers every base, with options for beginners right the way up to the best pros in the game, including the high-tech football boots worn by your heroes from the likes of Nike, adidas and PUMA. We also stock all the latest shirts and kits from hundreds of clubs and all the top international teams, ensuring you can wear your pride out loud. Whether you’re aiming to level up your own on-field performance or simply support your team, our football store has the products you need to do it.

At Westgress Sport, Players will:

  • Engage During The Summer
  • Be literate and numerate 
  • Learn to become active citizens 
  • Learn and develop the Saltley Outstanding Learner Qualities needed for success in life 
  • Prepare for and secure the foundations for future progression
